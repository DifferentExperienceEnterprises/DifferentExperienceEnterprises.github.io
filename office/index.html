Quality Products

Ceratizit is a leading manufacturer of cutting tools and wear parts, and we are proud to offer their products to our customers. We carry a range of Ceratizit solutions for boring, workpiece clamping, milling, threading, tool holders, and turning operations. These products are known for their exceptional quality and durability, and they are used in a wide range of applications across various industries.

TDK is a world-renowned electronics company, and we are honored to be one of their authorized distributors of Impeder Cores. Our range of TDK Impeder Cores includes the ZR series, ZRH series, ZRS series, ZRSH series, and ZRSH squared series impeder cores. These high-performance products are essential for induction heating applications, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible solutions.

JK Super Drive is a leading manufacturer of high-speed steel (HSS) cutting tools, and we are pleased to offer their products to our customers. Our range of JK Super Drive products includes HSS straight shank stub drills, long series drills, extra long series drills, taper shank drills, rolled forged drills, centre drills, and more. These products are known for their precision and reliability, and they are used in a wide range of metalworking applications.
